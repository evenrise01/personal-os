# Data Schema Reference

## Deep Work Hours
Focused time logged as distraction-free work.

## Skill Hours
Deliberate practice toward creative or design mastery.

## Workout Consistency
% of planned workouts completed.

## Stress Score
User-rated from 1–10.

## Client Profitability Score
Calculated from:
- Revenue
- Time spent
- Revision count
- Stress score

## Revenue per Deep Work Hour
Revenue / Deep Work Hours

Used to detect leverage.
